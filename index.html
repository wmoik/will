Traditional Expertise

With her roots in Oriental medicine and culture, Yoshiko brings with her the authentic and traditional shiatsu massage therapy and relaxation techniques to America. Yoshiko came to America in 1980 and has two decades of experience providing massage therapy to her clients.

Health and Well-Being

Yoshiko has always held a strong interest in complete full body health, believing that an acute injury is the cause of multiple interacting systems in the body. She believes nutrition, exercise, as well as full body maintenance and relaxation is needed to keep the mind and body in harmony.

Family

Shiatsu. Acupuncture delivered through finger pressure.

Shiatsu is a traditional Oriental message therapy technique that utilizes pressure points to increase energy flow through Meridian Flow Pathways. These techniques are meant to break apart the blocks that form through repititive daily actions and tense muscles. These techniques are similar to that of acupuncture, which target the meridian points with needles.

Foot Reflexology. An ancient practice.

This treatement is based on the belief that the entire body is connected via the nervous system and every part of the foot is connected to another part of the body. It's goal is to clear the pathways of energy flow, return the body to it's original state, and increase the immune response. It does so by stimulating the lymphatic system to eliminate waste.

Bamboo Fusion Massage. Swedish massage with heated bamboo rollers.

The therapist will incorporate heated bamboo to roll and knead your muscles. This method allows the heat from the bamboo to relax muscles as it kneads and rolls. This allows the therapist to penetrate your muscles more deeply with less pressure.
